ZugZug the game



How to play


You have to kill the monsters to win. Its simple but fun!

  • Start the game with "startup.py".
  • Control menu -> control the player.
  • When you touch the enemy sprite the combat window appears.
  • Use the corresponding buttons to deal damage or defend yourself.
  • Make maps with the map editor.
  • If you had enough just close the game.
