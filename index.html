About me

Hello, I'm Balázs Mészáros, a passionate full-stack developer with a strong inclination towards frontend technologies like JavaScript and React.js.

While my expertise lies in crafting responsive and interactive user interfaces, I also possess the skills to work with backend technologies such as C# and Python. Over the years, I've honed my coding skills and delved into the intricacies of web development, gaining hands-on experience in building robust and responsive web applications.

Apart from coding, I stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in technology, from cutting-edge processors to powerful GPUs. Exploring the ever-evolving tech landscape is both a passion and a hobby for me. I like to make logos and edit images with photoshop or illustrator. I'm also an avid gamer who revels in immersive gaming experiences, competing in epic battles, and uncovering captivating stories. When it's time to unwind, I indulge in the magic of cinema. I'm equally at home watching classic movies or tuning into gaming-related streams on platforms like Twitch. Exploring the wide spectrum of content on YouTube is another source of entertainment and inspiration.

These diverse interests not only enrich my life but also influence my approach to problem-solving and creativity in the digital realm. I'm a firm believer that a well-rounded perspective enhances every aspect of my work as a developer.
